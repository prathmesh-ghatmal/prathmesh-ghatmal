## 🚀 Featured Projects

### 🧩 [MahaCrossword (Gamified Crossword Platform)](https://github.com/prathmesh-ghatmal)
* **Tech Stack:** `Flutter` · `Node.js` · `Express.js` · `Python` · `PostgreSQL` · `MongoDB` · `Hive` · `AWS`
* Architected and developed a cross-platform gamified crossword platform (Android, iOS, Web, and Admin Panel) to digitize MahaCrossword, a record-holding Marathi crossword, enabling interactive solving through Grid, List, Category, and Leaderboard experiences.
* Engineered a high-performance crossword engine capable of rendering and navigating grids exceeding 133,000 cells (1330 × 100) with optimized pan/zoom interactions, offline persistence, and sub-100ms answer validation powered by a custom Marathi language validation engine.
* Built a multilingual crossword authoring system with PDF/Excel exports, cloud synchronization, and scalable backend services, enabling users to create, manage, and publish crosswords.

### 🏫 [MES Shikshan Prabodhini ERP](https://github.com/prathmesh-ghatmal)
* **Tech Stack:** `Next.js` · `Node.js` · `Express.js` · `PostgreSQL`
* Led the development of a multi-tenant ERP platform that digitizes school administration by automating admissions, examination management, teacher operations, academic records, extracurricular activities, and competitive examination workflows.
* Designed scalable role-based access control (RBAC), centralized data management, and workflow automation to streamline operations across multiple educational institutions.

### 🏆 [Navonmesh.online (Hackathon Management Platform)](https://github.com/prathmesh-ghatmal)
* **Tech Stack:** `React` · `Node.js` · `Express.js` · `MongoDB`
* Developed a centralized hackathon management platform that automated participant registration, payment collection, identity verification, project submission, topic allocation, evaluation workflows, result publishing, certificate generation, and post-event feedback collection.
* Built role-based dashboards for participants, judges, organizers, and administrators while streamlining the complete lifecycle of large-scale hackathons.
